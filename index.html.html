4H
Macro Bias — The Filter
Determines your trade direction. Never fight this timeframe.
Step 1 of 4 — Direction Filter
You are only allowed to trade in the direction this timeframe dictates. If 4H is bearish, all lower TF long setups are IGNORED.
Key Concepts
V
VWAP Direction
Is price above or below VWAP? Slope direction tells you who controls fair value for the session.
Below + sloping down = Bearish
Above + sloping up = Bullish
MS
Market Structure
Track swing highs and lows. A ChoCH on 4H is the most powerful signal that bias has completely flipped.
LH + LL = Bearish trend
HH + HL = Bullish trend
PD
Premium / Discount
Find the 50% midpoint of the 4H range. Only sell in premium (above 50%). Only buy in discount (below 50%).
Premium = sell zone · Discount = buy zone
OB
Order Blocks
Mark the last bearish candle before a bullish expansion (and vice versa). These are institutional entry zones.
4H OB = highest quality zone

Rules
01
Never scalp against the 4H bias — a counter-trend setup with 10 confluences is still lower quality than an aligned setup with 5.The trend is your most powerful confluence.
02

03

1H
Structure & POI Mapping
Where will smart money react? Mark the zones, not entries.
Step 2 of 4 — Zone Identification
You are NOT entering trades here. You are identifying 1–2 key price zones where you will watch for 15M confirmation. Quality over quantity.
Key Concepts
VW
VWAP as SR
On 1H, VWAP acts as a dynamic support/resistance level. Institutions defend and attack this level systematically.
Price below VWAP rejecting upward = strong short zone
OB
1H Order Blocks
Last displacement candle before a significant move. Price will return to these. The high/low of the candle defines your zone.
Mark top and bottom — this is your reaction box
FVG
Fair Value Gap
Three-candle imbalance where the wicks don't overlap. Price is magnetically drawn back to fill these gaps before continuing.
Unfilled 1H FVG = high-probability retest target
BOS
BOS / ChoCH
Break of Structure confirms trend continuation. Change of Character signals a potential reversal — both must align with 4H bias.
1H ChoCH aligned with 4H bearish = very high conviction
Zone Quality Ranking
S+
1H OB + FVG overlap at VWAP levelThree confluences at the exact same price — rarest and most powerful zone type.
A
1H OB with BOS confirmation above/belowStructural context adds significant conviction to the order block.
B
Standalone 1H FVG in the direction of 4H biasValid zone but lacks structural confluence — watch carefully.
C
Support/resistance level only — no SMC structureLowest quality. Only trade if supported by many 15M confluences.
15M
Precision Zone & VWAP Confluence
Refine the 1H zone into a tight entry window with VWAP as the anchor.
Step 3 of 4 — Entry Zone Refinement
This is where your setup fully forms. The 15M VWAP retest from the wrong side is the single most powerful entry trigger available to a scalper.
VWAP Retest Setup (Core Pattern)
1
Price breaks below 15M VWAP (for shorts) — this is the initial signal that sellers are taking control.Watch for the break candle — high volume = institutional.
2
Price retraces back up to test VWAP from underneath — this is the entry trigger zone.You want the retest, not the initial break.
3
VWAP retest coincides with a 1H OB or FVG — this is the nested confluence that creates A+ setups.One zone on top of another = highest probability.
4
Volume Delta turns negative during the retest — confirms institutions are selling into the bounce.Positive delta during a short retest = warning sign, wait.
Nested Confluence Map
NZ
Nested Zones
A 15M OB sitting inside a 1H FVG = "zone inside a zone." Price hitting this area faces double institutional resistance.
Adds +1 point to setup grade
LQ
Liquidity Sweep
Equal highs/lows on 15M are stop-loss pools. When price sweeps through them and reverses, smart money has cleared the path.
Sweep + reversal = smart money fingerprint
VD
Volume Delta
Cumulative buy vs sell volume. Negative delta on a bounce = sellers aggressive. Confirms the retest is being defended.
Negative delta on short zone = confirmed
RSI
RSI Context
RSI at 60–70 on a retest in a downtrend = momentum has recovered but not reversed. Divergence adds extra conviction.
Bearish divergence at zone = highest RSI confluence
5M
Execution — Entry Triggers
Everything above told you where and why. This tells you when.
Step 4 of 4 — Pull the Trigger
You never enter on a 4H or 1H signal alone. You wait for the 5M to give you a trigger INSIDE the zone. Patience here separates professional traders from gamblers.
Entry Triggers — select all that apply
Bearish / Bullish Engulfing Candle
Candle body fully engulfs the prior candle. Body close matters — not just wick. High-volume engulf = strongest version.
5M ChoCH (Change of Character)
Micro structure shift inside the 15M zone. A 5M ChoCH in the direction of the 4H trend is the highest quality execution trigger.
Liquidity Sweep + Close Back Inside
Price spikes through equal highs/lows grabbing stops, then immediately closes back inside range. Enter on the close of that reversal candle.
RSI Divergence on 5M
Price makes a new swing extreme inside the zone but RSI fails to confirm. Momentum exhaustion. Best paired with another trigger.
